About This Group

Welcome to the Business Group — a community for students who want to explore business, startups, and entrepreneurship beyond the classroom. Since IB doesn't directly teach business, this is our chance to learn hands-on — whether it's launching an online shop, planning a micro-bakery through Bolt, or exploring marketing and management tools.

We'll work together, delegate roles, learn new skills, and support each other's personal projects or team initiatives. The aim is to gain real-world experience while having fun building something meaningful as a group.

If we get more than 4 people, we'll host an introductory meeting where members can vote to revise or stick with the current goals and ideas.

Meeting Structure & Schedule

We plan to meet once per week, or more if needed, to brainstorm, organize, delegate tasks, and track progress on group or individual business projects.

Additional Details

Contact: WhatsApp group link will be sent via email

Organization: A shared workspace with planning resources and task delegation will be introduced in the first meeting

Meeting Times: Will be decided collectively after reviewing everyone's availability; most likely weekly

Location: Will be set based on convenience — potentially in the library or any quiet planning space

CAS Relevance

This group has strong potential to count as Creativity, Service, or even Activity for CAS — especially with documented contributions, teamwork, and leadership. We'll check the specific CAS rules to confirm anything unclear.

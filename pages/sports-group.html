About This Group

Welcome to the Sports Group — a space for anyone who wants to stay active, motivated, and engaged through movement. Whether it's volleyball, running, climbing, or other cost-free activities, this group is about fun, health, and building discipline together.

You don't need to be an athlete — just show up with the right attitude. Sessions will be casual but consistent, and we'll focus on motivation, improvement, and bonding as a team. It's not about competition — it's about activity, mindset, and community.

If we gather more than 4 interested people, we'll hold a kick-off meeting to collectively discuss or revise the group goals based on everyone's expectations.

Meeting Structure & Schedule

We aim for weekly or biweekly sessions, depending on activity type and participant availability. Sessions will rotate based on interest — one week could be volleyball, another week a park run, etc.

Additional Details

Contact: WhatsApp group link will be sent via email

Organization: Organization will happen live in the group — no formal materials

Meeting Times: Will be selected based on applications; typically once a week or every two weeks

Location: Will change depending on the activity (e.g., park, sports field, indoor gym)

CAS Relevance

This group is a great candidate for the Activity strand in CAS. Regular attendance and participation can count, and we'll seek more info where necessary.
